About me

Currently, I am a Phd student in Division of Decision and Control Systems, KTH Royal Institute of Technology (Kungliga Tekniska högskolan). My supervisor is Prof. Håkan Hjalmarsson .I am broadly interested in theories of control, systems and information. My past research focused on fuzzy control and descriptor systems. My current research is mainly about subspace identification, statistical learning and data-driven control.

Education

  • M.S. in Control Engineering from Tsinghua University in July, 2021.

  • B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Tsinghua University in July, 2018.

Research Interests

  • System Identification, with focus on subspace identification

  • Statistical Learning, with focus on finite-sample analysis

  • Data-driven Control
